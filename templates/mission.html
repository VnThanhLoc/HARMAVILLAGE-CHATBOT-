Our Mission

A word from the CEO himself

We started PharmaVillage because every Vietnamese should have access to safe, affordable medicines. If you don't have insurance or have a high deductible plan, you know that even the most basic medications can cost a fortune. Many people are spending crazy amounts of money each month just to stay healthy. Nobody should have to suffer or worse - because they can't afford basic prescription medications.

Our goal is to dramatically reduce the cost of drugs like Albendazole, but we also think that it is just as important to introduce transparency to the pricing of drugs so patients know they are getting a fair price.

Your trusted partner in pharmaceutical distribution, connecting providers with patients across Vietnam.
